# Level 39: Addition (Up to 1000)

**Class:** 3–4 | **Age Group:** 8–10 years

## **Objective**

Develop fluency in adding numbers up to 1000 using place value, with and without regrouping.

## **Description**

Students learn to add one-digit, two-digit, and three-digit numbers using vertical addition. 

They apply place value concepts to solve addition problems with and without regrouping and gradually build confidence in adding numbers of increasing complexity.

## **Learning Outcome**

Students can:

* Add numbers up to 1000\.  
* Solve addition problems with and without regrouping.  
* Apply place value while adding.  
* Add numbers with different digit lengths.  
* Verify addition answers.

## **Topics Covered**

* Addition without Regrouping  
* Addition with Regrouping  
* Vertical Addition  
* Place Value  
* Mixed-Digit Addition
